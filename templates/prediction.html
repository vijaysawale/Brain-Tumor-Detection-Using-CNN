Malaria Detection System

Malaria parasites can be identified by examining under the microscope, a drop of patient's blood is spread out as a "blood smear" on a microscope slide.
By uploading a clear image of RBC cells of the patient, our application will predict whether the person is infected or not.
For this we are using 3 layer architecture of CNN model. By how much percentage the person is infected will be mentioned, if the result is positive .
Also the test and precautions the patient must take, will be stated in the result.
The model has limitations and cannot be used for definite clinical predictions! Instead it can be used for research and comparison purposes (please see notes below).




Notes :

This deep learning model has been trained and validated, using the following dataset: The Datascience Company
The suitability of this deep learning model for clinical use has not been validated.
Therefore it cannot be used for any clinical purpose! It can be used for research purposes and for comparison with other similar models.
